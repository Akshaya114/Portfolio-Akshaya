A LITTLE ABOUT ME

akshaya kani

  • Tamil Nadu, India
  • 15th March, 2003
  • Shiv Nadar Uni, 2020-2024

I have always been fascinated by the way a simple ball of yarn or a tin of paint can be transformed into a something breathtaking. Over the last 5 years, I worked extensively with thread, striving to make unique works of art.

I usually translate an image into a piece of fabric craft by a technique called macramé. It involves using a variety of knots on thread, string and rope to create functional and decorative items.
